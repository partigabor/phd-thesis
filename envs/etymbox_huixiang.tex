\begin{etymology}\label{ety:huixiang}
\textbf{Mandarin Chinese} \traditionalchinesefont{茴香} \textit{huíxiāng} `fennel' [hui-spice], \traditionalchinesefont{茴} \textit{hui} refers to fennel, anise, and related plants; it perhaps could be rendered as `muslim spice' by way of its construction: \traditionalchinesefont{回} `Islam; Hui people' + \traditionalchinesefont{艹} `grass, herb' (Hu, 2005); or, phono semantic compund\footnote{\textcite{hu_food_2005}}
\end{etymology}
